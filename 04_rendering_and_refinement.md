# Module 04 – Rendering & Refinement

## Purpose
Assemble all validated content into final structured output while enforcing formatting rules.

---

## Main Responsibilities
- Enforce word limits: 100–150 words per section summary
- Generate:
  - Paper Summary
  - Section-by-Section Table
  - Expert + Lay Summaries
  - Mini-Glossary
  - Final Checks & Warnings list

---

## Formatting Rules
- No new information outside of provided text
- Harmonize acronyms and terminology
- Reuse defined terms consistently
- Maintain professional tone (no emojis)

---

## Refinement Strategy
Keep content priority:
1. Objectives  
2. Methods  
3. Findings  
4. Limitations  

Remove redundant or unclear sentences.

---

## Output
Structured final report with verified correctness and compliance.
